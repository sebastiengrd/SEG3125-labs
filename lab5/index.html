Our Services

General Care

Our professional staff can offer a variety of services including medical treatement for eye, skin and several diseases.

Dentist

We can answer your questions regarding dental concerns and do preventive care to make sure your dog and cat will always be smiling to you. We can also treat a lot of dental-related diseases. We have all the latest equipment necessary to take care of your animal.

Emergency

We always have one veterinary surgeon on call. We can take care of any emergency any time because. The health and safety of your animal is our priority.

Surgical Care

Our experienced veterinarians surgeons can provide a wide variety of surgical care.

Information Session

You never had an animal and you want one for the first time? You are wondering what having an animal requires? What are the financial costs? Come at our information sessions to learn all the basics of having an animal and to answer all of your questions!
